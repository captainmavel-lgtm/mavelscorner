Three years ago, I didn't think I'd make it through that season. I had lost everything that gave me a sense of identity, and I didn't know how to begin again.

But God met me in the rebuilding. Piece by piece, He restored what I thought was gone for good, and showed me that my story wasn't over. It was just being rewritten.

If you're in your own season of rebuilding right now, know this: you are not forgotten, and you are not finished.
